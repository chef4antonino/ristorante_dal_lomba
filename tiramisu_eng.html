TIRAMISU'

DESCRIPTION

Tiramisu is a sweet, spread throughout the Italian territory. It is a dessert based on ladyfingers dipped in coffee and covered with a cream, composed of mascarpone, eggs and sugar and in our variant there is also marsala. This dessert has a very recent origin in fact it was induced in the sixties in Veneto or Friuli Venezia Giulia.


INGREDIANTS:

  • 6 egg yolks and 3 egg whites
  • 500g mascarpone cheese
  • 50g sugar
  • savoiardi q.s.
  • coffee q.s.
