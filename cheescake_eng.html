STORY

Many are convinced that the original recipe is English, but not everyone knows what the true origin is and we are sure that discovering it will surprise you.
Where was the cheesecake born and who should we thank for this sweet invention?
The first cheese cake we have memory of was served to athletes during the Olympic Games. But we are not talking about the modern Olympics in Atlanta or London, but about the ancient ones that took place in Greece.
In fact, in the eighth century A.C., according to what Callimachus tells us, there was a man, Egimio, who had dabbled with the drafting of a text entirely dedicated to the art of making cheese cakes, ancestors of the cheesecake cake.
The passion of the Greeks for cheesecake is also confirmed by historians who tell us that, in 776 a.C., on the island of Delos athletes were refreshed with a cake made with sheep's cheese and honey.
