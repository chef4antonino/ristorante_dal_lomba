TONNARELLI CACIO E PEPE

DESCRIPTION

The tonnarelli cacio e pepe are a great classic of Roman-Lazio cuisine, they have now become a symbol - together with carbonara, gricia and amatriciana - of the city of Rome. This first course with a few simple ingredients, has poor origins and comes from the pastoral tradition of Lazio; the shepherds in fact, when they went to the mountains during the grazing period, they used to bring seasoned cheese, dried pasta and black pepper, which gave off heat and helped them to warm up.
