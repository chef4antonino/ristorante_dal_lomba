PISTACHIO CRUSTED PORK FILLET

Description

Pistachio crusted pork fillet is a really surprising second course. Fillet is a very tender and easily chewable meat. In this recipe it's filled with pistachios and covered with a crunchy and tasty breading. Pistachio's crispy texture makes this dish one of the most required, able to delight even the gourmands.

Ingredients

  • 150 g of crusted pork fillet
  • 15 g of chopped pistachios
  • 10 pistachios
  • 15 g of breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • half an onion
  • herbs: rosemary, thyme, marjoram
  • white wine
  • salt
  • pepper
