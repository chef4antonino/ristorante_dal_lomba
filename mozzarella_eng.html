MOZZARELLA IN CARROZZA

DESCRIPTION

Typical appetizer of Neapolitan cuisine. If mozzarella is one of your favorite food, then you chose the right dish

INGREDIENTS

  • flour
  • 5 eggs
  • 12 slices of white bread
  • breadcrumbs
  • 1 buffalo mozzarella of 250g
  • groundnut oil for frying
  • a pinch of salt
