CAPRESE

DESCRIPTION

The caprese salad is a cold appetizer typical of Campania and in particular of the island of Capri. It is a simple salad but it is freshness, lightness and flavor have made it such a famous dish that it is commonly called "a caprese" omitting the term salad. Summer recipe par excellence, the caprese is served both as an appetizer and as a main course.

INGREDIANTS

  • buffalo mozzarella
  • round tomatoes
  • basil
  • salt
  • extra virgin olive oil
