MIXED GRILLED MEATS

DESCRIPTION

The mixed grilled meat is a timeless bomb of flavors, an inviting variety of meat to be cooked according to taste: rare or well done. It should be prepared on important, special occasions, and the choice of the type of meat is subjective: for our version we have chosen sausages, bacon, chops, pork ribs and lamb chops. If you like strong flavors, accompany it with chimichurri, the famous Argentine sauce made with parsley and chilli.

INGREDIENTS

  • Spareribs
  • Pork chops
