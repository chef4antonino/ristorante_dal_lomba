PAPPARDELLE AL CINGHIALE

DESCRIPTION:

Pappardelle with wild boar ragù are part of the culinary tradition of the Tuscan Maremma ... tasty and appetizing, they contain the authentic taste of the past. After the long hours of cooking, this fine meat will be soft and it will be difficult to resist the scent of the freshly made ragù

INGREDIANTS:

  • ground boar
  • celery
  • carrot
  • onion
  • rosemary
  • garlic
  • sage
  • oil
  • vegetable broth
  • white wine
  • salt
  • pepper
  • juniper berries
